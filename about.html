About Learnify

Transforming education through interactive learning

Our Mission

At Learnify, we believe that education is the most powerful tool to change the world. Our mission is to make learning accessible, engaging, and effective for everyone. We strive to create an environment where students can explore complex concepts through interactive lessons, practice their knowledge with targeted quizzes, and track their progress over time.

Accessible Learning

Making quality educational content available to everyone, anywhere, anytime

Interactive Experience

Creating engaging content that encourages active participation

Measurable Progress

Helping students track their improvement and stay motivated

Our Story

Learnify started as a small project at Shubhashree College in Baneshwor, Kathmandu. A group of passionate educators and developers came together with a shared vision: to transform how students engage with educational content.

What began as a simple collection of PDFs and quizzes has evolved into a comprehensive learning platform that helps students master complex subjects through interactive content. Today, we're proud to serve students across the region, providing them with the tools they need to succeed in their educational journey.
